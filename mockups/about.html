About Collection Keeper

What is Collection Keeper?
Collection Keeper is a virtual collection of coins and stamps for each user. Anyone can sign up for free and can start collectiong coins and stamps online. The coins and stamps you collect are not real objects, but it makes a fun online hobby. Through Collection Keeper you can interact with different users from around the world who are all collecting coins and stamps just like you.
